Action points: each unit gets 4 action points.

Attack action points
       2      base normal attack
       -1     Hasted
       +1     Crippled
       +1     Winded
       +2     Slowed

Hitting your Target
When you attack your target, you role one dice to check to see if you hit.  The defender also rolls a dice to see if he can succesfully evade the attack.
  -1   If experienced
  -1   If blind
  0    Proficient
  +1   If Expert
  +1   If Master

Archer perks
Sniper:		+1 to hit, if held in possition for 1 turn.
Sitting Duck:	+1 tp hit, if target held in possition for 1 turn.
